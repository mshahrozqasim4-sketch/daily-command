# LAUNCHPAD OS

> A discipline tracking app for founders, builders, and operators who run things.

Built by M Shahroz Qasim — solo founder, D2C brand builder, Lahore, PK.

---

## What it does

LAUNCHPAD OS is a mobile-first PWA that keeps you disciplined and focused on your mission. No fluff. No cute animations. Just a terminal-style command center for your day.

- **Live clock** with day counter from your journey start
- **Mission countdown** to your launch date
- **Daily alarms** with push notifications
- **Habit tracker** with streak counting
- **28-day consistency grid**
- **Activity log** — every action timestamped
- **Full settings** — everything customizable, no code needed

---

## Fully customizable

Open the Settings tab and configure:
- Your name, city, mission, company
- Your launch / target date
- Your journey start date
- Your own alarms (add, edit, delete)
- Your own habits (add, edit, delete)

Everything saves locally on your device. No account. No server. No tracking.

---

## Deploy your own (5 minutes, free)

**GitHub Pages**
1. Fork this repo
2. Settings → Pages → Branch: main → Save
3. Live at `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/launchpad-os`

**Android APK**
1. Deploy to GitHub Pages first
2. Go to pwabuilder.com
3. Paste your GitHub Pages URL
4. Click Android → Download Package
5. Install APK on your phone

**Add to home screen**
Open in Chrome → Share → Add to Home Screen
